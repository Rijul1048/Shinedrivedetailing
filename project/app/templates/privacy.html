Cookies and Analytics

Cookies are small text files placed on your device that help the website function and improve your experience. We use the following categories:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: required for core functionality (e.g., page navigation, basic security). The site cannot function properly without these cookies.
  • Preference Cookies: remember your choices, such as selected vehicle type or previously entered form values during a session.
  • Analytics Cookies: help us understand site usage (e.g., pages visited, interactions) so we can improve performance and content. We use aggregated reports; these do not directly identify you.

You can manage non-essential cookies using the consent banner on our site or by adjusting your browser settings to block or delete cookies. Disabling some cookies may impact site performance or features.

Learn more about controlling cookies at allaboutcookies.org.
