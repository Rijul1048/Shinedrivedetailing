Frequently Asked Questions About Exterior Car Detailing

Mobile exterior car detailing is our professional car exterior cleaning service that comes directly to your location. Our fully-equipped team brings all necessary tools, equipment, and premium products to your home, office, or preferred location to perform complete exterior detailing services with the same quality as a traditional detailing shop.

Exterior car detailing service times vary by package and vehicle size:

  • Bronze Exterior Detail: 1-2 hours
  • Gold Exterior Detail: 2-3 hours
  • Platinum Exterior Detail: 3-4 hours
Larger vehicles (SUVs, trucks) may take additional time for a complete exterior car detail.

We service all of Waterloo Region including Kitchener, Cambridge, and surrounding areas. While we primarily focus on Waterloo, we may be able to accommodate locations further away with a small travel fee. Contact us with your address and we'll let you know if we can service your area for exterior car detailing services.

We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling of exterior car detailing appointments. Appointments cancelled with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a $25 cancellation fee. No-shows will be charged 50% of the service price.

The main differences are convenience and location. Mobile exterior detailing means we come to you with all our equipment, saving you time. The quality is identical - we use the same professional techniques and products. The only limitation is that some services requiring paint curing (like ceramic coatings) need appropriate weather conditions or shaded areas when performed mobile for complete exterior car detailing.

We offer flexible service packages to accommodate different budgets for exterior car detailing. Many clients start with essential services and add more treatments over time as their budget allows.
