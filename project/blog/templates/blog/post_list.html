What is professional car detailing?

Professional car detailing is a comprehensive cleaning and restoration process that goes beyond a regular car wash. It includes thorough cleaning of both interior and exterior surfaces, paint correction, protection treatments, and attention to every detail.

How often should I get my car detailed?

For daily drivers in Waterloo, Kitchener, and Cambridge areas, we recommend professional detailing every 3-4 months. High-end vehicles or those in harsh Ontario weather conditions may need more frequent service.

What's the difference between car wash and detailing?

A car wash focuses on basic cleaning, while detailing is a comprehensive process that includes deep cleaning, paint correction, interior deep cleaning, and protection treatments.

Do you offer mobile car detailing in Elmira, Woolwich, and St. Jacobs?

Can detailing remove scratches from my car?

Professional detailing can significantly improve or remove light scratches through paint correction techniques. Deep scratches may require more extensive repair.

How long does a full detailing service take?

A basic detail typically takes 2-3 hours, while a comprehensive full detail can take 4-6 hours. Premium packages with paint correction may require a full day.
